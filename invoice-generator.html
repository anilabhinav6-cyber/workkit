Invoice numbering best practices

A good invoice numbering system is sequential, unique, and consistent. Most businesses use a prefix (INV, 2026, client initials) followed by a padded number (INV-0001). Always keep records in order — gaps in invoice numbers can raise questions during tax audits.

Common formats

  • INV-1001 — simple sequential
  • 2026-001 — year-based restart
  • CLIENT-001 — per-client numbering
  • INV-2026-001 — year and sequence
