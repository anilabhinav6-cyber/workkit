How to split a PDF

Upload your PDF, type which pages you want (single pages like "5", ranges like "1-3", or a mix like "1-3, 7, 10-12"), and download a new PDF containing only those pages in that order. The original file is untouched. This is ideal for pulling one chapter out of a book, separating an invoice from a bundle, or removing confidential pages before sharing.
