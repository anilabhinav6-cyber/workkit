GST slabs in India

  • 0% — fresh vegetables, milk, eggs, unbranded staples
  • 5% — packaged food, medicines, transport, small restaurants
  • 12% — computers, processed foods, business-class air travel
  • 18% — most services: IT, telecom, financial services, restaurants
  • 28% — luxury cars, tobacco, aerated drinks

CGST, SGST and IGST explained

For sales within one state (intrastate), GST splits equally: 18% GST = 9% CGST (central) + 9% SGST (state). For sales between states (interstate), the full amount is charged as IGST. The total tax is identical either way — only the split differs.

How to remove GST from a price

To find the base price from a GST-inclusive amount, divide by (1 + rate). Example: ₹1,180 inclusive of 18% GST → ₹1,180 ÷ 1.18 = ₹1,000 base + ₹180 GST. Simply subtracting 18% gives the wrong answer.
